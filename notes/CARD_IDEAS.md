


- frenzy: all cards cost 1 less energy for 4 seconds

- shiv: 0-cost, dash to an enemy, dealing damage.
- shuriken: 1-cost, dash to an enemy, dealing damage. if kills an enemy, gain 2 energy

- rogue: slash attack. Make the next 2 attack cards free.

- undertow: shadow-dash, deal damage to enemies you pass through (like pyke E)



## MECHANICS:
- multiple-players
- temporary: after X seconds, this card is discarded
- auto-play: when a card is drawn, instantly play it
- reducing/increasing energy
- shift-to-discard:  If players hold LSHIFT and press ability, they discard the card instead of playing it
- recast: sometimes, cards may put an ephemeral-card on top of the stack
